Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for resources?
You can use our search bar on the homepage. Filter by town, distance, category, or keywords to quickly find the resources you need.
Can I suggest a new organization or program?
Yes! Scroll to the bottom of the homepage and use the “Contact Us” form to submit new resources or programs to be added.
Is there a mobile-friendly version?
Absolutely. Our website is fully responsive, so you can access it from any device, including phones and tablets.
Are all resources verified?
We strive to list only trusted and verified organizations. We regularly update information and encourage users to report outdated details.
How can I volunteer?
Check our “Volunteer / Get Involved” page for opportunities, or contact the organization directly through the provided links.
