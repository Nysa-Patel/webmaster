Grow it Green Winter Market

Grow It Green Morristown hosts the Winter Market every Sunday from December through April at the Convent Train Station. Local farmers, food producers, and artisans come together to offer fresh produce, meats, eggs, baked goods, cheeses, prepared foods, and handcrafted items. The market fosters a welcoming community space where families and neighbors can shop local and support small businesses all winter long!

3/29 Morris County Empanada Trail Run

Join us for a scenic run in Morris County on Sunday March 29th! Experience the trails of Central Park of Morris County on the Cross Country Course. After your run, enjoy a unique finisher medal and a delicious empanada (vegetarian options available). Your registration includes a shirt, medal, and empanada. We hope to bring families and friends together for fitness and food while giving back to our community. We are committed to supporting breast cancer research and organizations that help give support to breast cancer patients and advancing athritis research.
